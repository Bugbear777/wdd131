Site Plan: Vacationing in Sedona, AZ

Site Name

Name: RedRockRetreat.com

This name was chosen to reflect the stunning red rock scenery that defines Sedona, Arizona, and the calming, rejuvenating experience of visiting the area. It evokes both a visual and emotional connection to the destination.

Optional domain availability: redrockretreat.com (subject to registrar check)

Site Purpose

This website is a personal travel guide highlighting the natural beauty and memorable experiences of vacationing in Sedona, AZ. It will include:

  • An About page sharing my personal vacation story and inspiration for the site
  • A Things To Do page outlining top attractions, hiking trails, local dining, and more
  • A visual and inviting Homepage introducing Sedona with images and highlights

Scenarios

  • What are the must-see spots and hikes for a first-time visitor to Sedona?
  • Where can I find local dining or relaxing spa experiences near the red rocks?

Color Scheme

  • #A63A50 – Red Rock Accent (used for headings, buttons, and highlights)
  • #FAF3E0 – Soft Desert Sand (used for page background)

Typography

  • EB Garamond – Used for main body text and paragraphs for an elegant, readable look
  • Montserrat – Used for headings and navigation for a clean, modern touch
