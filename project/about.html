About Our Company

Red Rock Retreat is a Sedona-based travel service dedicated to helping visitors make the most of their time in Arizona’s red rock country. Specializing in personalized trip planning, they connect guests with the area’s best experiences—whether that’s booking scenic Jeep tours, arranging guided hikes to iconic spots like Cathedral Rock and Devil’s Bridge, or recommending hidden-gem trails away from the crowds.

Our Mission

To inspire and guide every visitor to experience the beauty, culture, and adventure of Sedona by providing expert recommendations, seamless bookings, and personalized itineraries that turn a simple trip into an unforgettable journey.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and sought-after guide to Sedona, celebrated for connecting travelers with the region’s iconic landscapes, hidden treasures, and transformative experiences—fostering a lifelong love for Arizona’s red rock country.

Joseph Marlow | Our CEO

Joseph Marlow is the founder and CEO of Red Rock Retreat, bringing a deep passion for Sedona’s landscapes, culture, and outdoor adventures to every guest experience. With a background in hospitality and a lifelong love of exploring the Southwest, Joseph built Red Rock Retreat to help travelers discover the best of Sedona—whether that’s a breathtaking sunrise hike, a hidden local café, or a soul-soothing wellness retreat. Known for his warm, approachable style and dedication to personalized service, Joseph leads the company with a simple philosophy: every visitor should leave Sedona with unforgettable memories and a desire to return.
